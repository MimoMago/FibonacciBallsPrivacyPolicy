Suika Slice Privacy Policy (Last Updated: 2023-11-19)

Mimo Mago Productions (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is the developer of the mobile game application “Suika Slice.” This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you about the data collection practices related to our game.

Information Collection and Information Use

We do not collect any personal information from you when you play our game. We do not use any information about you for any purpose.

Ads

Our game uses AdMob to display ads. AdMob is a third-party advertising service that may collect certain information about you, such as your IP address, device type, and app usage data. AdMob uses this information to personalize the ads you see. You can learn more about AdMob's privacy policy here: AdMob Privacy Policy

If you want to opt out of personalized ads, please visit Google Ad Settings.

Please note that AdMob's data collection is governed by Google's privacy policy, which you can review here: Google Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

We do not collect or share any personal information or data from users. The only data collection in our game is related to AdMob for ad-serving purposes, as described above.

Children's Privacy

"Suika Slice" is designed for general audiences and is not specifically intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately at support@mimomago.uk. We will take steps to remove the information and any associated data from our records.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any changes, we will post the updated Privacy Policy on the Suika Slice Privacy Policy Website.

This policy is effective as of 2023-11-19.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@mimomago.uk.
